Frequently Asked Questions - Yuba City Criminal Defense

Where is the Sutter County Superior Court located?

The Sutter County Superior Court is located at 1175 Civic Center Blvd, Yuba City, CA 95993. This modern courthouse, opened in 2014, handles all criminal, civil, and family law matters for Sutter County residents.

What criminal charges does Tony Carlos defend in Yuba City?

Tony Carlos defends all criminal charges in Yuba City including DUI/DWI, drug crimes, theft, assault, domestic violence, felonies, misdemeanors, and probation violations. He also handles expungements to clear past convictions from your record.

What should I do if I'm arrested in Yuba City?

If you're arrested, exercise your right to remain silent and ask for an attorney immediately. Do not discuss your case with police without legal representation. Contact Tony Carlos Law as soon as possible at (530) 645-2551.

Can a criminal record be expunged in California?

Yes, many criminal convictions in California can be expunged under Penal Code 1203.4. This can help with employment, housing, and professional licensing. Tony Carlos can evaluate your case to determine if you qualify for expungement.
