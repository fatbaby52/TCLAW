Legal Representation for Olivehurst Residents

Olivehurst is a census-designated place in southern Yuba County, located just south of Marysville along Highway 70. If you're facing criminal charges or need immigration assistance, Tony Carlos Law provides experienced legal representation to Olivehurst residents.

As a former prosecutor with over 15 years of experience in Yuba County courts, Tony Carlos understands the local legal system and can provide the aggressive representation you need. Our Yuba City office is just a short drive from Olivehurst, making it convenient to meet and discuss your case.

Criminal Defense Services for Olivehurst

Criminal cases for Olivehurst residents are heard at the Yuba County Superior Court in Marysville. Tony Carlos defends all types of criminal charges including:

  • DUI/DWI - Highway 70 traffic stops and DUI checkpoints
  • Drug Crimes - Possession, sales, and manufacturing charges
  • Theft Crimes - Shoplifting, burglary, robbery
  • Violent Crimes - Assault, battery, domestic violence
  • Probation Violations - Defense against revocation
  • Expungements - Clearing past convictions
