Tony Carlos — Yuba City Criminal Defense Attorney

Former Sutter County Prosecutor | J.D., Pacific McGeorge | California State Bar #267547

Joseph Anthony “Tony” Carlos is a criminal defense attorney based in Yuba City, California, and a former Sutter County Deputy District Attorney. He represents people accused of crimes throughout Sutter County and Yuba County — handling DUI, domestic violence, felony and misdemeanor charges, probation violations, warrants, restraining orders, and post-conviction matters.

He earned his Juris Doctor from the University of the Pacific, McGeorge School of Law in 2009, and was admitted to the California State Bar the same year (Bar #267547).

From Prosecutor to Defender

Tony began his legal career as a Deputy District Attorney at the Sutter County District Attorney's Office, where he prosecuted criminal cases on behalf of the State of California. He now uses that experience on the other side of the courtroom — defending people accused of crimes.

Having worked as a prosecutor, Tony approaches each case the way the government does. That perspective helps him evaluate how charging decisions are made, read police reports critically, weigh plea offers, identify witness and evidence problems, and plan trial strategy. It is one part of building a defense — every case still turns on its own facts and the law that applies to it.
